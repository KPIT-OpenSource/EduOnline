KPIT eduOnline is an e-learning platform that offers interactive online courses for the employees.

The EduOnline allows the employees to access course content including videos, textbooks, and problems,

and to check their progress in the course. The EduOnline also offers a forum for discussion and a wiki

open to both employees and course team members to contribute. For course team members,

the EduOnline includes an Instructor Dashboard, with options for employees to enroll, produce reports, and

administer a course while it runs. One can access and use the EduOnline directly through a browser.
